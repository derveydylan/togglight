Togglight is a puzzle game where you toggle lights (that are on a grid) between off and on states.
When switching a light state, it reverses the neighboring lights states too, in a plus shape (+).
A puzzle is resolved when all lights are off.

Unlike other Lights Out games, the grid can contain cells without lights.

Gamemodes

Basic Levels

Basics levels are manually generated levels to resolve. Each level has a certain difficulty and introduces you to the game mechanics.

Infinte

Infinte levels are levels that regenerates indefinitely, on a fixed grid size.
